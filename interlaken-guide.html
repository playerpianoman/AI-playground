🏔️ Fun Things to Do in Interlaken, Switzerland 🏔️

Your Ultimate Guide to the Adventure Capital of the Swiss Alps

Discover breathtaking mountains, pristine lakes, and thrilling adventures in one of Europe's most beautiful destinations!

Welcome to Interlaken! Nestled between the stunning Lake Thun and Lake Brienz, surrounded by the majestic peaks of the Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau, Interlaken is truly a paradise for adventure seekers and nature lovers. Whether you're looking for heart-pumping thrills or peaceful mountain moments, this Swiss gem offers something magical for everyone.

🪂 Adventure & Adrenaline Activities

Paragliding

Soar like a bird over the Swiss Alps! Experience breathtaking aerial views of Interlaken, both lakes, and the surrounding mountains. This tandem flight with certified instructors is perfect for beginners and offers an unforgettable perspective of the region.

Skydiving from Helicopter

For the ultimate adrenaline rush, jump from 14,000 feet with the Swiss Alps as your backdrop. This helicopter skydive offers unparalleled views and an experience you'll never forget.

Bungee Jumping

Take the leap of faith from 132 meters high! Jump towards Lake Stockensee and experience the thrill of free-falling in one of the world's most beautiful locations.

Canyon Swing in Grindelwald

Experience a 90-meter thrilling jump into Glacier Canyon. This heart-stopping adventure is not for the faint of heart but provides an incredible rush for thrill-seekers.

Canyoning

Navigate through narrow gorges, slide down waterfalls, and jump into crystal-clear pools. Explore the Chli Schliere or Saxeten Canyon for an all-day adventure through Swiss nature.

🏔️ Mountain Excursions & Scenic Railways

Jungfraujoch - "Top of Europe"

Take the historic railway to Europe's highest train station at 3,454 meters. Enjoy glacier views, the Ice Palace, and panoramic vistas of the Alps. This UNESCO World Heritage site is a must-visit!

Harder Kulm

Ride the funicular to Interlaken's "house mountain" for spectacular panoramic views. The Two Lakes Bridge viewing platform offers stunning vistas of both lakes and the Jungfrau massif.

Schynige Platte Train

Journey on this historic cogwheel train (operating since 1893) through wildflower meadows to enjoy breathtaking alpine views and visit the beautiful botanical garden.

Pfingstegg Toboggan Run

Experience a thrilling alpine coaster ride down winding tracks with epic views of the Swiss Alps. Control your own speed on this family-friendly adventure in Grindelwald.

🚤 Lake Activities & Water Adventures

Lake Brienz Boat Tours

Cruise the turquoise waters of Lake Brienz and admire the stunning mountain reflections. Take a jet boat for thrills or a scenic cruise for relaxation.

Lake Thun Exploration

Enjoy boat trips on the deep blue waters of Lake Thun, visit charming lakeside villages, and marvel at the surrounding Alpine scenery. Perfect for a peaceful day on the water.

Paddleboarding & Kayaking

Paddle the crystal-clear waters of Lake Brienz at your own pace. The lake's incredibly clear, turquoise water provides a refreshing break from mountain adventures.

Chocolate Fondue Boat Tour

Combine Swiss chocolate with scenic lake views! Enjoy delicious chocolate fondue with fruits, marshmallows, and wine while cruising Lake Brienz.

🥾 Hiking & Nature Exploration

Augstmatthorn Ridge Hike

One of the most spectacular ridge walks in the Alps with jaw-dropping views. Choose between the challenging shortcut from Lombachalp or the traditional route from Harder Kulm.

Lauterbrunnen Valley Walk

Stroll through the "Valley of 72 Waterfalls" on this easy, family-friendly walk from Stechelberg to Lauterbrunnen. Marvel at towering cliffs and cascading waterfalls.

Oeschinensee Lake Hike

Visit one of Switzerland's most beautiful alpine lakes with stunning turquoise waters surrounded by dramatic cliffs. Take the cable car from Kandersteg for easy access.

Trümmelbach Falls

Explore ten glacier-fed waterfalls inside a mountain! Take the historic tunnel-funicular built in 1913 to witness this incredible natural wonder.

🏰 Cultural & Unique Experiences

William Tell Open-Air Theatre

Experience Swiss culture at this historic outdoor theatre in the Rugenwald forest. In 2025, enjoy "Robin Hood" - the first new production in over 100 years!

Grindelwald Village

Visit this fairytale-like village with its charming chalets, turquoise lakes, and stunning mountain backdrop. Perfect for escaping crowds and experiencing local Swiss culture.

St. Beatus Caves

Explore over a kilometer of underground caves featuring stalactites, stalagmites, and underground waterfalls. Learn about the legend of St. Beat and the mythological dragon.

Oberhofen Castle

Visit this 13th-century fairytale castle sitting on the shores of Lake Thun. Explore medieval rooms, beautiful gardens, and enjoy stunning lake views from the towers.

🍫 Swiss Culinary Experiences

Swiss Fondue

No visit to Switzerland is complete without trying authentic cheese fondue! Visit Fondue Villa & Garden, Alti Metzg Bistro in Mürren, or Restaurant Laterne for the full experience.

Swiss Chocolate Tasting

Indulge in world-famous Swiss chocolate at Läderach or visit the Funky Chocolate Club for workshops and tastings. Learn about the chocolate-making process!

Fondue Cooking Class

Learn to make Switzerland's national dish with hands-on cooking classes. Discover the secrets of perfect fondue and Swiss culinary traditions.

🎿 Winter Wonderland Activities

Visit in winter for skiing, snowboarding, ice climbing, night sledding, and winter hiking in a magical snowy landscape!

📅 Best Time to Visit

🌞 Summer (June-September)

Perfect for hiking, paragliding, lake activities, and mountain excursions. Warm weather and all facilities open, but expect crowds.

❄️ Winter (December-March)

Ideal for skiing, snowboarding, winter sports, and cozy mountain experiences. Magical snow-covered landscapes.

🍂 Spring/Fall (April-May, Oct-Nov)

Fewer crowds, lower prices, great for sightseeing. Some high-altitude activities may have limited access.

💡 Essential Tips for Your Interlaken Adventure

  • Book adventure activities in advance, especially during peak season
  • Consider getting the Swiss Travel Pass for unlimited access to trains, buses, and boats
  • Pack layers - mountain weather can change quickly
  • Tie your shoes tight before paragliding (trust us on this one!)
  • Allow 4-5 days to experience the main highlights without feeling rushed
  • Download the SBB Mobile app for easy train travel throughout Switzerland
  • Try the local specialties - fondue, raclette, and Swiss chocolate are must-tries!
  • Bring a camera with extra batteries - the scenery is incredibly photogenic
